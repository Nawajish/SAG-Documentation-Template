Adobe and Software AG Partnership

Adobe and Software AG share a common vision of delivering the best possible customer experiences through open, robust customer engagement platforms. As the only Integration vendor in the Open Data Initiative (ODI) Partner Advisory Council, Software AG is driving this open standard and its adoption. Adobe and Software AG are partnering to help businesses transform their customer experience management (CXM) by bringing together customer data from across multiple enterprise systems into a centralized and actionable real-time customer profile. The two companies achieve this with integrations between Adobe Experience Platform and Software AG’s webMethods Integration and API management services.
