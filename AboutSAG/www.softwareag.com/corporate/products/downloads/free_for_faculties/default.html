Market-oriented education gives graduates entering the job market an excellent head start and offers an ideal foundation for academic research projects. Software AG provides market-leading software for business processes, integration, intelligent business operations and transactions.

Free software is at the core of our University Relations Program. But education means more. STREAMING ANALYTICS EDUCATION PACKAGE APAMA | MASHZONE NEXTGEN Learn more

OVERVIEW

The free of charge Streaming Analytics Education Package (SAEP) is designed to be a full-term independent study project, which provides students with real life stock market data requiring them to handle and analyse real world event streams. The SAEP contains Software AG´s product platform for Intelligent Business Operations (IBO) enabling informed decisions to be made at speed and scale by integrating real-time analytics and decisioning into transaction executing systems.


TRAINING MATERIAL

The University Relations Team offers free training material, along with a complete scenario to follow, and other valuable resources.


DURATION

The first step of the SAEP is the set-up of the software and getting familiar with the scenario. This step takes about 4 hours. The completion of the required tasks is the second step. To perform the tasks in different system environments, Software AG offers the SAEP in to various versions:

- Thebasic version: Students need ≈ 2 hours to complete the provided tasks.
- Theadvanced version: Students need ≈ 4 hours to complete the provided tasks.


SELF-EXPLANATORY ENVIRONMENT

All required software is either pre-installed or pre-configured. If the user does need to manually install the program, this process will be explained in detail and can be performed by any user with administrator rights.


SUPPORT

The University Relations Team offers a variety of opportunities to review and discuss issues if needed, such as a forum, a blog, documentation and a FAQ page. However, the University Relations Team does not provide personal support for this free-of-charge offering.

INTEGRATION EDUCATION PACKAGE WEBMETHODS Learn more

OVERVIEW

The free of charge Integration Education Package (IEP) is designed to be a full-term independent study project, enabling students to integrate provided applications and freely available web services in an SOA. The result is a cutting-edge and process-driven campus management system for a virtual customer—the “University of Almamater.” The package includes interviews with the university’s staff and students, which allow the project participants to analyze requirements while at the same time, identify their tasks.


TRAINING MATERIAL

The University Relations Team offers free training material, along with a complete scenario to follow, and other valuable resources.


SELF-EXPLANATORY ENVIRONMENT

All required software is either pre-installed or pre-configured. If the user does need to manually install the program, this process will be explained in detail and can be performed by any user with administrator rights.


DURATION

The Integration Education Package is shaped to fit the practical part of a lecture and takes approximately 12 weeks when the user spends 90 minutes per week working on the package.


SUPPORT

The University Relations Team offers a variety of opportunities to review and discuss issues if needed, such as a forum, a blog, documentation and a FAQ page. However, the University Relations Team does not provide personal support for this free-of-charge offering.

ARIS EDUCATION PACKAGE ARIS Learn more

OVERVIEW

The free of charge ARIS Education Package (AEP) is designed to provide the easiest way to make ARIS available for education purposes of leading universities and business schools. AEP was developed to be used as a supplement to business process management lectures. The packages are divided into exercise blocks of approximately 90 minutes each.


TRAINING MATERIAL

The University Relations Team offers free training material, along with a complete scenario to follow, and other valuable resources.


SELF-EXPLANATORY ENVIRONMENT
All required software can be downloaded via a FTP link and the user will get detailed installation information about the whole installation procedure.

DURATION

ARIS Education Package is shaped to fit the practical part of a lecture and takes approximately 12 weeks when the user spends 90 minutes per week working on the package.
