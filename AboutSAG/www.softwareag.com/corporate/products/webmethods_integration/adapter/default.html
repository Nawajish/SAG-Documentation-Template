WEBMETHODS ADAPTER INTEGRATION: CONNECT WITHOUT CODING!

 
Quickly link your on-premises systems and applications to the webMethods hybrid integration platform with 300+ of field-proven adapters for packaged applications, databases and legacy systems. Simply plug them in — no coding required.

We’ve kept it simple by offering a common development framework and common runtime infrastructure for all adapters. Adapters share common user interfaces and design approaches. Custom development’s also easy with APIs that speed up development and simplify event-driven apps and resources.

For security and compliance purposes, the framework also provides centralized journal and audit logging capabilities so you can track and monitor the health of the system, as well as who is accessing what application for compliance reporting.
