GET TO KNOW Software AG Bulgaria

Our Bulgarian office was established in 2006 and grew up significantly over the past 13+ years. In Sofia we employ 250+ people in various departments such as R&D, Professional Services, Global Information Services, Cloud Service Operations, Security & Compliance, Digital Marketing etc. and have established ourselves as the third largest R&D center of the company. We are a place of great minds and cutting edge-technologies, and we work together to solve business problems that impact people’s lives.
